For the Shopping Cart I used this Design Patterns

1) COMPOSITE: Component(Item), Leaf(Product) and Composite(Offer).
2) STRATEGY: Strategy(Payment), ConcreteStrategy(CreditCard, Paypal, Cash), Context(Order).
3) SINGLETON: TransctionNumber.
4) ITERATOR: ShoppingCartItem.
5) OBSERVER: Subject(Item:Offer and Product, Transaction), Observer(MailList)
